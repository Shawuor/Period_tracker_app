A simple, private app to track your menstrual cycle and predict future periods. All data stays on your device.


[Click here to start](https://periodtrackerapp-divoqxfjtvzd7apphesc8a.streamlit.app/)

## **Quick Start**
1.  Install: `pip install -r requirements.txt`
2.  Run: `streamlit run Period_tracker_updated.py`
3.  Use: Log periods, view stats, get predictions.

## **Key Features**
*   **Log periods** with symptoms/notes
*   **View charts** of your cycle trends
*   **Predict** next period & fertile window
*   **Export/import** your data (CSV)
*   **100% private** - no data leaves your browser
